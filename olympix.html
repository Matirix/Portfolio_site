Olympix

In this group project, our team was tasked with creating a mobile web application that centered around the Olympics. What kind of needs might arise that we could develop an app to solve it.

We decided to tackle the issue of accessibility. Since the Olympics is an event that is intended for all audiences of varying ages, it should also be made accessible to everybody. Streaming sites that host Olympics may not be friendly to the new user as they are bombarded with an overwhelming amount of information. We solve this issue by centralizing all of the Olympic's events and streams into a minimalistic site that gets them connected to the stream upon landing on the page.
